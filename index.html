Neighborhoods

The Coachella Valley, explored.

From Palm Desert's family-friendly streets to Indian Wells' prestige enclaves — we know every corner.

Our Home Base

Palm Desert

Palm Desert is the heart of the Coachella Valley — a thriving city with world-class dining, retail, and culture. El Paseo, the "Rodeo Drive of the Desert," anchors a community where luxury living meets everyday convenience.

Home to top-rated schools, the McCallum Theatre, and some of the valley's most desirable residential neighborhoods.

Desert Luxury

La Quinta

La Quinta is where the desert meets distinction. Home to PGA West, the La Quinta Resort, and some of the valley's most prestigious gated communities, it draws buyers who want privacy, prestige, and world-class amenities.

Old Town La Quinta adds an arts-and-culture dimension with galleries, restaurants, and farmers markets.

Prestige Living

Indian Wells

Indian Wells is the valley's most exclusive enclave — a small, quiet city known for stunning mountain views, manicured fairways, and a remarkably low crime rate. Home to the BNP Paribas Open.

With some of the most coveted addresses in the desert, Indian Wells attracts discerning buyers who value privacy and uncompromising quality of life.
